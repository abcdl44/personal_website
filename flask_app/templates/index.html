Who am I?

My name is Wesley Chen, and I am currently a third year computer science student at the University of Maryland, College Park. Thanks for taking the time to drop by my site. Be sure to check out the projects tabs to see what stuff I've been up to lately. If you're here as part of job related evaluation, I hope I can make a decent showing of myself!

Who am I? Minus the superficial description

I'd like to describe myself as passionate, especially when it comes to making other people happy. I love making personal connections with others, and tend to really value these relationships once they've been established. These relationships are true regardless of whether they were made in person or online - some of my favorite people are those I've met through the internet, both through gaming and other activities.

Speaking of which, gaming happens to be one of (if not my favorite) hobby. I love all kinds of games, though my favorite would definitely include social based MMOs. It's a lot of fun meeting different people from all over the world, and learning about different cultures and kinds of people through the freedom of the internet has always been a cool experience. Lately I've been looking forwards to the western release of Lost Ark, so fingers crossed that it comes out on time!

I'd love to turn my passion for playing games into work related use, and work as a full time game designer or developer. I feel that as a long time gamer, I can understand what sort of things make people happy, and what mechanics and behaviors are less welcomed by players both casual and competitive.

If you're still here, thanks for taking the time to read all this. Have a nice day, both anonymous strangers and to-be-met acquaintances!
