Initium

Initium was a game I created content for while in high school, and was my first practical experience with game design. It's an indie text based web MMO, and had a very tiny but loyal community when I played. Working and playing it helped inspire my passion for game design, as I got to directly see the reactions of the playerbase as they encountered and explored my work. It would be a dream to work on an MMO like Initium some time in the future, and to contribute to something which could make many kinds of players happy.
